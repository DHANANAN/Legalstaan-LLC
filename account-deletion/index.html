Delete Your Legalstaan Account & Data

Last updated: 2026-05-08 · App: com.legalstaan.app

You can permanently delete your Legalstaan account and all associated data.

How to Request Deletion

Email from your registered Google account to:

krishnashelkeintern@gmail.com

Subject: Delete my Legalstaan account

Body: Include the email address you signed up with.

We confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Firebase Authentication account
  • Cloud Firestore profile (name, photo, course progress, streaks)
  • Community posts and direct messages you authored
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging push token
  • Uploaded profile photo
  • Local AI chat history (uninstall the app)

What Is Retained

  • Anonymized analytics events (cannot be tied back to you after deletion)
  • Crash reports up to 90 days, anonymized
  • Legal records as required by Indian law

Stop Collection Without Email

  1. Sign out (Profile → Sign Out)
  2. Uninstall the app
  3. Revoke at myaccount.google.com/permissions
